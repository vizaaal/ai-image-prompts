# 🌲 Ultra-Realistic Foggy Forest Background

## 📖 Description

Transform any portrait into a breathtaking cinematic masterpiece by replacing the background with a lush enchanted forest covered in dense natural fog. The result features rich green tones, atmospheric depth, soft volumetric lighting, and a premium editorial look while preserving the original subject exactly.

---

# 📝 Prompt

Use the uploaded photo as the exact reference.

**Do NOT change the person's face, facial features, skin tone, hairstyle, haircut, beard, expression, body proportions, clothing, pose, camera angle, framing, or direction of view. Preserve the subject's identity exactly.**

Replace only the background.

Create an ultra-realistic dense foggy forest environment with towering pine and cedar trees covered in lush emerald-green foliage. Fill the entire background with soft natural white mist to create a mysterious cinematic atmosphere.

Add subtle volumetric sunlight rays filtering through the trees, creating realistic depth and atmospheric perspective. Introduce layers of distant trees fading naturally into the fog.

Apply premium forest-inspired cinematic color grading using:

- Deep Emerald Green
- Moss Green
- Dark Teal
- Earthy Brown
- Soft Neutral Whites

Enhance realism with detailed tree bark, forest floor textures, scattered rocks, fallen leaves, soft grass, and slight moisture on surfaces.

Blend the subject naturally into the environment with realistic ambient green light reflecting onto the clothing and subtle environmental shadows.

Maintain accurate perspective, realistic depth of field, natural contrast, HDR dynamic range, crisp details, and lifelike lighting.

The final image should resemble a professional outdoor editorial photograph captured using a full-frame DSLR with a 50mm prime lens.

---

## ❌ Negative Prompt

Do NOT generate:

- Cartoon
- Anime
- Painting
- CGI appearance
- Plastic skin
- Fake lighting
- Oversaturated colors
- Low resolution
- Blurry details
- Duplicate people
- Distorted anatomy
- Extra limbs
- Text
- Watermark
- Logo
- Artifacts
