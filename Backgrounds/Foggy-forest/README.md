# 🌿 Ultra-Realistic Jungle Adventure Portrait

## Prompt

Use the uploaded image as the exact reference for the person.

Preserve the person's identity with maximum accuracy, including facial features, skin tone, hairstyle, facial hair, expression, body proportions, clothing, accessories, pose, framing, camera angle, and natural perspective.

Create a photorealistic scene where the person appears to be standing naturally in the middle of a dense tropical rainforest, as though the photograph was genuinely captured there by a professional outdoor photographer.

Surround the person with towering rainforest trees, lush green foliage, hanging vines, moss-covered rocks, dense vegetation, and soft white morning mist. Add subtle sun rays filtering naturally through the forest canopy, creating realistic atmospheric depth.

Place a calm, non-aggressive snake naturally near the person's arm or shoulder as if it is peacefully interacting with the person during a wildlife photography moment. The snake should appear anatomically correct, naturally positioned, and consistent with the lighting and perspective of the scene.

The lighting must be physically accurate and consistent across the entire image:

- Soft diffused daylight from above through the forest canopy.
- Natural shadows falling consistently across the face, clothing, vegetation, and ground.
- Realistic green ambient light reflected subtly from surrounding foliage.
- Accurate contact shadows where the subject meets the forest floor.
- Natural atmospheric haze between foreground and background.

The skin tone must remain completely natural, matching how real human skin appears under shaded forest lighting. Preserve authentic skin texture, pores, subtle imperfections, and realistic color variation. Avoid artificial beauty filters, oversmoothing, excessive sharpening, HDR halos, or exaggerated saturation.

Ensure that clothing naturally responds to the forest lighting and atmosphere. Hair edges, clothing edges, and fine details should blend seamlessly with the environment without visible cut-out borders or compositing artifacts.

The final result should look like a genuine high-end wildlife portrait captured on location using a professional full-frame DSLR camera with a 50mm prime lens. The image should be indistinguishable from an authentic outdoor photograph.

## Negative Prompt

Do NOT generate:

- Artificial AI appearance
- Background replacement artifacts
- Cut-out edges
- Halo around the subject
- Plastic skin
- Beauty filters
- Fake lighting
- Unrealistic shadows
- Floating subject
- Cartoon
- Anime
- Painting
- CGI look
- Unrealistic snake anatomy
- Blurred face
- Low resolution
- Oversaturated colors
- Watermarks
- Logos
- Extra limbs
- Distorted body proportions

Create a photograph that convincingly looks like it was captured during a real jungle expedition, with authentic lighting, natural skin tones, realistic wildlife interaction, and a premium cinematic adventure aesthetic.
